Comprehensive Ocean Safety Platform

Empowering Indian coastal communities with advanced technology for real-time hazard detection and emergency response

Community Reporting

Enable fishermen, coastal residents, and maritime workers to report ocean hazards instantly through our multilingual mobile interface supporting Hindi, English, and regional languages.

Social Media Analytics

Monitor Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, and local social platforms for ocean hazard mentions using AI-powered sentiment analysis and keyword detection in multiple Indian languages.

Emergency Alerts

Instant SMS, WhatsApp, and push notification system integrated with Indian Coast Guard and state disaster management authorities for immediate community warnings.

Geographic Intelligence

Advanced GIS mapping with satellite imagery integration, covering all Indian coastal states from Gujarat to West Bengal, including island territories.

Mobile-First Design

Optimized for low-bandwidth connections and feature phones, ensuring accessibility for all users including remote fishing communities across India's coastline.

Emergency Response

Direct integration with Indian Coast Guard, Navy, NDRF, and state emergency services for coordinated rescue operations and disaster response.

How JAL SETU Works

Simple steps to contribute to ocean safety and receive critical alerts for your community

1

Report Hazard

Fishermen, coastal residents, and maritime workers can quickly report ocean hazards through our mobile app or SMS in their local language.

2

AI Analysis

Our AI system analyzes reports, cross-references with social media data, weather patterns, and validates information for accuracy and severity.

3

Alert & Response

Verified alerts are instantly sent to affected communities and emergency services, enabling rapid response and potentially saving lives.
